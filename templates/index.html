Raspberry Pi Smart Irrigation Controller

Never worry about your plants again!

Features



  • Automatic Watering

    The smart irrigation controller waters your plants automatically and precisely. The device uses capacitive soil moisture sensors to measure the capacitance of the soil. It uses this data to determine whether to activate the pump and water the plant.

  • Good for the Environment

    People often water too much or too little and end up wasting water. The smart irrigation controller solves that problem by watering with a pump. The settings for the pump can be adjusted and changed, so no water is wasted.

  • "Smart"

    The "Smart" Irrigation controller is smart. It can be monitored through this website, where you can view the temperature, soil moisture level, and when your plant was last watered. The customizable settings allow you to change how long to water, and the soil moisture level to water at. On top of all this, you can water your plants using the website.
